### 🎯 About Me

I'm a builder who lives at the intersection of **AI models** and **production systems**. Currently a grad student at **Northeastern**, shipping things people actually use.

- 🚀 **Incoming SDE Intern @ Amazon** — joining the Search team in Palo Alto, Summer 2026
- 🥗 **Co-Founder @ [Supatrack](https://supatrack.app)** — AI-powered nutrition tracking, live on iOS & Android with a 4.9+ rating
- 🔬 **Multimodal AI Research** — built [derm-platform](https://www.derm-platform.me/), a three-stream skin cancer detection system (vision + text + dermoscopy features) hitting 0.76 macro F1
- 🌐 **System Architect** — past work on India's ONDC government commerce grid, microservices at scale
- 📚 **Currently learning** — distributed systems internals, CLRS, and how Amazon Search actually works at scale

> 🟢 **Open to Fall 2026 internships and Spring 2027 new grad SWE roles**
